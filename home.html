Who

We at Amaryllis Cafe are passionate about community and coffee. We aim to serve our customers with ease and the comforts of a hot cup of coffee (if that's your cup of tea).

Why

A coffee break is a short mid-morning rest period granted to employees in business and industry, usually lasting from 10 to 20 minutes and frequently occur at the end of the first third of the work shift. Coffee breaks may take place away from the actual work-area in a designated cafeteria or tea room.
Whatever your coffee break is, get it where you want it.

What

Coffee delivered straight to your home or office. Our drinks taste great wherever you drink them, crafted to perfection by our master baristas. Good coffee can be hard to find, and we don't think you should be confined. No more coffee runs, whats not to love?
